Crypto Generates High Returns

Bitcoin is amazing at generating high returns. When people invest, their goal is to make money.
I am comparing Bitcoin with Vanguard 500 Index. Vanguard 500 Index takes the Top 500 companies and will put your money that you invest into each of the 500 companies.
It will invest based off the market cap. For example, a larger percent of your money is going to the companies with the higher market cap, such as Apple, Microsoft, and Google.
I used Portfolio Visualizer to compare how much I would make if I, theoretically, put $1,000 into Bitcoin and Vanguard in 2009 and sold that money today.
This is when the first Bitcoin was “mined” into existence. The results were shocking. I would have only had $2,461 with Vanguard, while I would have more than $78,000 with Bitcoin.
While Bitcoin had some down years, that was expected since the currency and coin were both new and this kind of investing scared people.
Eventually, the supply started to decrease and eventually become scarce, and the price increased.
